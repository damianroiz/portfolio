About

I got first exposed to web development when trying to set up a fitness blog on WordPress back in 2016. I never felt I had control about how my little blog looked in this platform, I became obsessed with learning everything I could about web-based solutions that could could create great user interatiions. I believe that things that look beautiful must also be functional and work great. This is my principle in every project I develop.

I take pride in saying that everything I've learn as a developer has been due to countless self-teaching hours on free platforms such as FreeCodeCamp, FullStack Open, Udemy, among others..., as well as official documentation from different technologies. Having the freedom to test and apply new technolgies in order to continuing delivering innovative solutions to my clients has always been a great source or motivation.

Therefore, if you are looking to increase business activity through solid web solutions that could exceed the expectations of your customers, contact me.
